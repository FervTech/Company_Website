WHAT IS RPA?

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is a technology that automates repetitive and rule-based tasks using software bots.

Automation through robots can perfectly mimic the behavior of a human worker in repetitive and tedious tasks such as transferring information from emails to spreadsheets, copying phone numbers, and scanning images or documents. These tasks demand precise eyes and hands to avoid errors in the digitization, not to mention lengthy hours of arduous human labor, which is why automation through RPA allows people to be liberated from these tasks, so they can undertake functions that a robot cannot accomplish.
